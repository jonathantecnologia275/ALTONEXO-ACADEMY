2
Introduction · Introducción

Just as important as greeting someone is knowing how to end a conversation politely. "Goodbye" works at any time of day, unlike "Good night," which is only for bedtime.

Tan importante como saludar es saber terminar una conversación cortésmente. "Goodbye" funciona a cualquier hora, a diferencia de "Good night," que es solo para dormir.

3
Detailed Explanation · Explicación detallada
Formal
Goodbye

Used in professional settings, or when leaving for a long time.

Se usa en entornos profesionales, o al despedirse por mucho tiempo.

Informal
Bye

Used with friends and family, in casual, everyday situations.

Se usa con amigos y familia, en situaciones casuales.

6
Grammar · Gramática

"Goodbye" and "Bye" are interjections, just like "Hello" and "Hi" — no conjugation needed, ever.

"Goodbye" y "Bye" son interjecciones, igual que "Hello" e "Hi" — nunca necesitan conjugarse.
