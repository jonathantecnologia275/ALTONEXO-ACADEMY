Introduction · Introducción

After noon, English speakers switch from "Good morning" to "Good afternoon." This lesson teaches you exactly when the switch happens and how to keep a conversation going during midday.

Después del mediodía, se cambia de "Good morning" a "Good afternoon." Aprenderás cuándo ocurre el cambio y cómo mantener una conversación al mediodía.

3
Detailed Explanation · Explicación detallada
Good afternoon
Used from 12:00 PM until about 6:00 PM

It's more formal than "Hi" and is common in offices, stores, and phone calls.

Es más formal que "Hi" y es común en oficinas, tiendas y llamadas telefónicas.

Pronunciation · Pronunciación
good af-ter-NOON
LUNCH

Stress falls on the last syllable of "afternoon" — af-ter-NOON. / El acento cae en la última sílaba: af-ter-NOON.

6
Grammar · Gramática

Like "Good morning," this is a fixed phrase. Just replace "morning" with "afternoon" — the structure never changes.

Al igual que "Good morning," es una frase fija. Solo se reemplaza "morning" por "afternoon."
