Introduction · Introducción

Ordinal numbers describe position or order — first place, second floor, third of May. They're essential for dates, rankings, and floors of a building.

Los números ordinales describen posición u orden — primer lugar, segundo piso, tres de mayo. Son esenciales para fechas, rankings y pisos de un edificio.

First, second, and third are irregular — the rest follow a "number + th" pattern. / First, second y third son irregulares — el resto sigue un patrón "número + th."

6
Grammar · Gramática

Most ordinal numbers add "-th" to the cardinal number (four → fourth). Only 1st, 2nd, and 3rd are irregular and must be memorized separately.

La mayoría de los ordinales agregan "-th" al número cardinal (four → fourth). Solo 1st, 2nd y 3rd son irregulares y deben memorizarse aparte.
