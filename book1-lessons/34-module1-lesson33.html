Introduction · Introducción

Knowing how to tell time is essential for schedules, appointments, and daily life. English uses two common ways to say the time: the "past/to" method and the digital method.

Saber decir la hora es esencial para horarios, citas y la vida diaria. El inglés usa dos formas comunes de decir la hora: el método "past/to" y el método digital.

Pronunciation · Pronunciación

In fast speech, "What time is it?" often sounds like "Whattimeizit?" — all connected. / En habla rápida, "What time is it?" suena como "Whattimeizit?" — todo conectado.

6
Grammar · Gramática

For minutes 1-30, use "past": "ten past three." For minutes 31-59, use "to" with the next hour: "ten to four." Always say "It's" before the time.

Para minutos 1-30, usa "past": "ten past three." Para minutos 31-59, usa "to" con la próxima hora: "ten to four." Siempre di "It's" antes de la hora.
