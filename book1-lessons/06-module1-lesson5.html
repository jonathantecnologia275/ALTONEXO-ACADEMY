2
Introduction · Introducción

Unlike "Good morning," "Good afternoon," and "Good evening," which are used to greet someone, "Good night" is only used as a farewell — right before someone goes to sleep or leaves for the night.

A diferencia de "Good morning," "Good afternoon" y "Good evening," que se usan para saludar, "Good night" solo se usa como despedida — justo antes de dormir o de irse por la noche.

3
Detailed Explanation · Explicación detallada
Good night
A farewell, not a greeting

You never use "Good night" when arriving somewhere — only when leaving or going to bed.

Nunca se usa "Good night" al llegar a un lugar — solo al irse o al ir a dormir.

Grammar · Gramática

"Good night" follows the same "good" + noun pattern, but functions grammatically and socially as a closing phrase, similar to "Goodbye."

"Good night" sigue el mismo patrón "good" + sustantivo, pero funciona como una frase de cierre, similar a "Goodbye."
