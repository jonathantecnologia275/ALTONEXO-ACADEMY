Grammar · Gramática

Many everyday expressions are idiomatic — their meaning isn't literal. "What's up?" doesn't ask about something above you; it means "How are you?"

Muchas expresiones cotidianas son idiomáticas — su significado no es literal. "What's up?" no pregunta sobre algo arriba; significa "¿Cómo estás?"
